About

The Digital Teaching Tool (DTT) is a service for teaching and learning about the principals of environmental monitoring using Satellite Remote Sensing. This tool is developed to support EUMETSAT's Copernicus training activities.

Legal Information

The DTT is based on the Vat System, which is developed by the Database Research Group of the University of Marburg (head: Prof. Bernhard Seeger). You can contact us at vat@mathematik.uni-marburg.de or visit our GitHub for further information.
